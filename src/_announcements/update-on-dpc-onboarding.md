The DPC team is actively working on a new DPC Portal. The Portal automates previously manual steps to request, get approved, and set up your systems for access to production data. Thanks to our research volunteers whose input helps shape this secure, streamlined experience.

## Onboarding and new requests for production access are still paused

Onboarding of existing users and new requests for production access are paused while we plan for the public launch of the Portal.

## While you wait: use the DPC sandbox

The DPC sandbox provides a realistic experience using synthetic beneficiary claims data.

[Request access to the sandbox](https://sandbox.dpc.cms.gov/users/sign_up)

## Join the DPC Google Group

Once you've set up a sandbox account, join the DPC Google Group. This active community lets users share tips, ask questions, and get help with troubleshooting.

[Join the DPC Google Group](https://groups.google.com/g/dpc-api)

## Stay in touch

If you have questions for us please write [dpcinfo@cms.hhs.gov](mailto:dpcinfo@cms.hhs.gov) or message us in the Google Group.
